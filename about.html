About Me

I’m a creative designer and UX Swiss army knife, with imaginative skills spanning from vision to execution. I delight in crafting beautiful experiences across the verticals of web design, app design, and human-computer interactions.

I thrive on the front lines of solving intensely human problems through great design. I work well in an environment that involves both open-ended blue sky projects as well as more focused flow and system thinking. Either way, I love the process as much as the finished product.

My playground is a wide array of work from rethinking educational to working towards solving problems related to climate change. I crave to be apart of teams and projects in which design and technology meet to create something that makes a true impact in the world.

Services

I partake in web design, app design, human-computer interactions and everything in between. My services include:

  • - UX research and user testing
  • - Website design and development
